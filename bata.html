Background
With rising shoe companies in the market, Bata is struggling to keep up. Bata has been around Malaysia for around 90 years. The tagline “First to Bata, then to school” has been long gone. Now, people think of Bata as “Buy And Throw Away”.
Objective
We want to remind Malaysians that Bata has once accompanied them through different stages of life.
Big Idea
Bata carries everyone’s memories. It is a shoe that was in some part of everyone’s life and captures our most precious moments. It is like your friend. A friend that knows you well. Bata Fits You Like A Friend.
