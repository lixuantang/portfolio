Background
Nutella has always been seen as just a spread for bread. Consumers are unaware of the possible usage of Nutella. This leads to a decrease in sales.
Objective
We want to push the boundaries of being just a spread for bread and show that Nutella is a very versatile snack that is fun to make and yummy to eat.
Big Idea
Thus, introducing “The New-tella Way” a.k.a “The New Way Of Eating Nutella”.
