Background
The Art Seni Tour is a curated tour that brings Malaysians to explore art through various galleries in Kuala Lumpur. Their goal is to bridge art and people together by focusing on its inaccessibility in Malaysia. However, many Malaysians are still not interested in joining the tour.
Objective
Malaysians are not connected to art. Thus, we want to turn non-art inclined people into infrequent, frequent and eventually into art-inclined people.
Big Idea
Art is more than just paintings or sculptures. It is an experience that you have never experienced in your daily life before. We will bring you to the world of Outrageous Wonders.
