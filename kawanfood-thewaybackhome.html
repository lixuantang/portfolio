Background
Malaysian perceive frozen food as unhealthy and bland in taste. They try to avoid buying frozen as much as possible because of this mindset. This hurts Kawan Food’s sales even though it is the leading Frozen food manufacturer in Malaysia.
Objective
We want to build brand awareness, strengthen the brand-consumer relationship and promote repeat purchase by changing their mindsets.
Big Idea
We gathered some insights on frozen food after tasting it, and most of them said that it does not have the frozen-food taste. It is just as good as home-cooked food. Kawan Food provides meals that reminds you of your mom’s home cook food. It brings you to The Way Back Home.
