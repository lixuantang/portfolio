Background
Kawan Food is Malaysia’s leading frozen Asian delicacies with Halal Certificate. Despite that, most Malay Gen Z and Millennials are not aware of KawanFood not to mention KG Pastry Glutinous Rice Balls.
Objective
We want to create brand salience amongst Malay Gen Z and Millennials by introducing them to KG Pastry Glutinous Rice Balls.
Big Idea
In order to understand our target audience, we held focus groups to let them test the product and gained useful insights which helped with our executions. Through the insights gathered, we found out that most of them agree that Kawan Food provides the yummiest glutinous rice balls that will get you hooked. You won’t be able to stop, cause “Mana Cukup?”. One is definitely not enough.
