Background
Tesla is a clean energy company that specialises in electric vehicles. It has not officially enter the Malaysia market thus is not as well known as the other car companies such as Perodua or Proton.
Objective
We want to create top-of-mind awareness of eco-friendly car-lovers. To instill the mindset in the target audience that there is a better car alternative that not only functions as a normal car but also saves the environment by running on electricity.
Big Idea
Tesla always has the latest technology and comes out with new ideas. Tesla will be the future car as it is always a step ahead from other car companies. It Leads The Future.
